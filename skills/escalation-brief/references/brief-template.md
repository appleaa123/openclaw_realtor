# Escalation Brief Template

## Format

```
🚨 ESCALATION BRIEF — [TENANT NAME] | [ADDRESS]
[Date]

1. SITUATION
[What is happening right now. Be specific: dollar amounts, dates, issue type.
Why does this need team attention today?]

2. CONTEXT
[How long have they been a tenant? Payment history (any past issues?).
Open maintenance issues? Total repair expenses to date. Any legal notices sent?]

3. ACTION NEEDED
[Specific ask: "Approve N4 filing", "Approve vendor expense of $X",
"Decide whether to renew lease", "Call tenant re: [specific issue]"]
```

## Example 1: Non-Payment

```
🚨 ESCALATION BRIEF — JANE DOE | 123 MAIN ST, TORONTO
09/03/2026

1. SITUATION
Rent unpaid for February and March ($4,200 total outstanding). Tenant has not responded to 3 emails sent in the past 2 weeks. Property marked late_payment.

2. CONTEXT
Tenant since Jan 2023 (3 years). First payment issue — no history of late rent previously. One prior maintenance claim (appliance, resolved). Total repairs to date: $850.

3. ACTION NEEDED
Approve N4 form filing for non-payment. N4 ready at /data/workspace/forms/[id]_N4.pdf — reply APPROVE to confirm.
```

## Example 2: Maintenance Escalation

```
🚨 ESCALATION BRIEF — BOB SMITH | 456 ELM AVE, MISSISSAUGA
09/03/2026

1. SITUATION
Tenant reported HVAC failure (no heat) on March 7. Two vendors contacted — both quoted $1,800–$2,400 for furnace replacement. Waiting on manager decision to proceed.

2. CONTEXT
Tenant since June 2021. Property HVAC last serviced 2019. Total repair expenses to date: $3,200. Tenant has been cooperative throughout.

3. ACTION NEEDED
Approve vendor choice and expense: Bob's HVAC ($1,800 + HST) or ProHeat ($2,400 + HST). Reply with vendor number to dispatch.
```

## Length Guideline

- Each bullet: 2-4 sentences
- Total brief: under 1,000 characters for WhatsApp delivery
- Use specific numbers, dates, dollar amounts — never vague language
